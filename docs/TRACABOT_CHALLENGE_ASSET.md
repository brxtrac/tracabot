# TRACaBot Community Challenge Asset

## Title

TRACaBot: DKG-Powered Community Safety

## Short Description

TRACaBot helps Telegram communities reduce scams by combining local moderation signals with OriginTrail DKG v10 Shared Memory. It watches for impersonation, fake airdrops, wallet-drain lures, suspicious joins, scam domains, scam wallets, and repeated campaign patterns. Evidence-backed reports and moderation outcomes become reusable fraud intelligence for other communities and OpenClaw agents.

## Easy Facts For Join Challenges

- Signal color: amber
- Memory phrase: shared trail
- Safety animal: lobster
- Checkpoint word: atlas
- Guard phrase: quiet graph
- Community marker: relay
- Friendly motto: stay on TRAC(k)

These fields are intentionally simple but not obvious from the bot name or group chat. A real person can find them quickly by opening the Knowledge Asset, while a spam account cannot pass by guessing `tracabot` or `telegram`.
